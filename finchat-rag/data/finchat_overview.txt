FinChat Overview

FinChat is an AI-powered financial assistant designed to help users analyze company performance, understand financial statements, and generate insights from market data.

Key Features:
1. Summarizes quarterly and annual reports.
2. Answers finance-related questions using retrieval-augmented generation.
3. Provides insights on revenue, profit, risk factors, and cash flow.
4. Helps users interpret financial metrics such as EPS, EBITDA, ROE, and Net Margin.

FinChat is mainly used by analysts who want quick explanations generated from corporate filings.
